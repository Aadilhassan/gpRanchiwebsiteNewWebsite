The Civil Engineering department envisions to build a strong teaching environment to achieve excellence in technical education providing highest standards of learning that transforms steadily to meet the challenges of 21st century.
Mission:
  1. To provide an open end environment that stimulates professional and personal growth of Proximate Stake holders i.e. students and faculty.
  2. To make our students competent enough in the fields of their career by giving more emphasis on Practical, Industrial, Computational aspects etc.
  3. To develop Knowledge in multi areas like Management, Design and Computer applications.
  4. To prepare leaders for diversified field of highly technological and information based Global Society.
